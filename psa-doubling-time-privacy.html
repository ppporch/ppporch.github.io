Privacy Policy for PSA Doubling Time Effective Date: May 1, 2026 PSA Doubling Time is designed to help clinicians estimate PSA doubling time from PSA date and value pairs. Data Collection PSA Doubling Time does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data. The app does not require an account. The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, cloud AI services, or external servers. PSA Values and Dates PSA values and dates entered into the app are stored locally on the user’s device. This information is not transmitted to the developer or to any third party. OCR and Camera Use The app includes an optional camera-based scan feature to help extract PSA values from lab tables. Text recognition is performed on device using Apple’s Vision framework. Captured images are used only for text recognition. Images are not saved to the user’s Photos library by the app and are not uploaded to any server. Clinician Verification Users are expected to review and verify all extracted PSA dates and values before using them for calculation. PDF and CSV Reports The app can generate PDF and CSV reports locally on the device. Sharing these reports is controlled by the user through the iOS share sheet. Health Information The app may be used to enter or process health-related information, but this information remains on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export or share it. Children’s Privacy This app is intended for use by clinicians and is not directed to children. Changes to This Policy This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s data practices change.
